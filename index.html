Warranty & Service

At Serene Audio we go to great extents to make sure our products are free of any manufacturing defects. Therefore, We warrant our products to the original retail purchaser against any failure resulting from original manufacturing defects in workmanship or materials.

All our speakers are backed up for two years from date of purchase from an authorized Serene Audio dealer.

Shipping & Returns

All orders ship within 5 business days of receiving the order directly from our shop in Vancouver.

We use FedEx or UPS Ground service.
Tracking number will be emailed to you when your speakers ship.

If you are not absolutely delighted with your speakers, you can return them to us in their original condition within 30 days of the date of delivery.
