What is it good for?

Talisman speakers are designed to sit on your desk, but they have plenty of power to fill the whole room. They are self-powered, meaning they can be paired with almost anything.

SOUND

For us, the reason that justifies the existence of Talisman is having exceptional sound quality. But every manufacturer says that, so here we back up our claim by getting a bit technical and showing you how we did it. Time to get your geek on!

Driver

Expensive ultra low distortion driver with fast transient response. This means plenty of space for each instrument and voice to present itself without masking or being masked by other instruments. Learn More

Enclosure

Wood, Leather, and Brass create a beautiful pallet of colors and textures that were meticulously selected with quality and durability as a priority. Learn More

Amplifier

Meticulously designed internal amplifier saves the integrity of the signal from the moment it enters the amp to the moment it is converted to acoustic waves by the drivers. Learn More

Workmanship

Our palette of materials consists of wood, leather, and metal. They provide a beautiful combination of colors and textures.

We maintain a high level of craftsmanship by a deep understanding of our materials, and by working with them patiently. We are sure when you hold one of our speakers in your hand, you will appreciate the quality. See More

Design

Our goal is to create products that induce visceral joy with every glance. As it is often suggested by people, we like to say that the Talisman design was inspired by the work of Antoni Gaudí, but that wouldn’t be the truth.

Talisman was born out of a few odd strokes of pen on a sketchbook, and so were many others, but Talisman was the design we fell in love with. It just looked and felt right.
