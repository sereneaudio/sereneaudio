Sound

You wouldn't want to step on a bridge, if its engineers told you it's standing there by magic, would you? As much as marketing departments of speaker manufacturers like you to believe, there isn't much magic in high performance speakers. Or at least you should hope so, or else you should question the competance of their engineers. Robert A. Heinlein said it best: “One man's ‘magic’ is another man's engineering.”

At Serene Audio we make magic happen by uderstanding what contributes a moving musical experience, and solving challeging engineering problems. Our research has proved to us there are three core characteristic that high performance speakers can't do without.

  1. Very low distortion of any kind

  2. Smooth and extended frequency response

  3. Wide sound dispersion
